Famous Shiva Temples

Beyond the revered Jyotirlingas and Dhaams, India is home to countless other ancient and magnificent temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Each tells a unique story of faith, architecture, and mythology, attracting millions of devotees.

1. Kailasa Temple

Ellora (Aurangabad), Maharashtra

An unparalleled architectural marvel, the Kailasa Temple is not built, but *carved* from a single, massive rock...

2. Murudeshwar Temple

Murudeshwar, Karnataka

Famous for its 123-feet (37m) tall statue of Lord Shiva, the second-tallest in the world, which sits majestically overlooking the Arabian Sea...

3. Amarnath Temple

Pahalgam, Jammu and Kashmir

One of the most sacred and challenging pilgrimages for Hindus, the Amarnath cave is located at an altitude of 12,756 feet...

4. Lingaraj Temple

Bhubaneswar, Odisha

A masterpiece of Kalinga architecture, the Lingaraj Temple is the largest and one of the oldest temples in Bhubaneswar...

5. Tungnath Temple

Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand

Believed to be over 1,000 years old, Tungnath is the highest Shiva temple in the world, located at an altitude of 12,073 feet...

6. Annamalaiyar Temple

Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu

This vast temple complex is dedicated to Lord Shiva, who is worshipped here as 'Arunachaleswara', representing the element of Fire...
